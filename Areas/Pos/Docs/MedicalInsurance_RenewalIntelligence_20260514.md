# Medical Insurance Renewal Intelligence - 2026-05-14

## Purpose

Renewal Intelligence makes insurance risk visible before it becomes an operational problem.

## Alerts Covered

- Renewals due this week.
- Renewals due this month.
- Expired policies.
- Suspended employees.
- Overdue installments.
- Missing payroll linkage.
- Missing documentation.

## UX Decisions

- Alerts are shown beside the membership and financial context.
- Urgency is indicated with clear status chips instead of raw technical messages.
- POS users can see and escalate, but cannot administer policies.
- MainErp remains the place for HR approval and setup.

## Demo Flow

1. Open POS Medical Insurance.
2. Show renewal due count.
3. Open Alerts Center.
4. Select an affected employee.
5. Show card, dependents, renewal date, and payroll-linked deduction.
6. Explain that MainErp handles renewal approval while POS gives branch visibility.

## Roadmap

- Renewal reminder notifications.
- Provider renewal batches.
- Missing-document workflow.
- Export renewal list to Excel/PDF.
