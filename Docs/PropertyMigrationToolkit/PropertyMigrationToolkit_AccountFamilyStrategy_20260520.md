## Strategy
Family detection is used to reduce manual review and suggest target parent accounts. It is not a substitute for finance approval when historical accounting journals are involved.

## RSMDB Finding
RSMDB can classify many accounts by name and usage, but Unknown remains the largest family. The next phase should focus on mapping high-usage Unknown accounts first.
